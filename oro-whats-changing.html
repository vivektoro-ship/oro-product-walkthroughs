Oro  ·  Project One  ·  Change Brief

What's Changing

Two flagship flows — the Branch-Led Gold Loan (BEAGLE) and the Takeover Engine — are being rebuilt to a single, low-friction, AA-integrated standard. This is the overview: why, what changes, and which apps it touches.

§

01 Why we're doing this

Both journeys had grown a set of manual, opaque, and disconnected steps. The revamp removes the friction, plugs the gaps, and brings the customer, partner, and ops experiences to parity — anchored on the Account Aggregator (Finvu) SDK for bank linking and Mendix for Federal prechecks and appraisal handling.

These are the friction points being removed:

  • Customers were gated behind an unnecessary Oro gold-valuation approval before they could even see their plan.
  • Federal precheck failures were opaque to partners — no error copy, no way to act on them.
  • Bank-account selection was a manual step disconnected from AA, on both the gold-loan and takeover flows.
  • Loan purpose wasn't captured at the point of loan creation.
  • Fund-transfer confirmation was entirely partner-driven — no automation even when AA consent existed.
  • Co-borrower eSign had no delivery or sharing mechanism.
  • Existing-Lender-Release (ELR) visits had no parent grouping, no search linkage, and no status visibility from the loan view.
  • The Oro appraisal was matched against the bank's sheet for no added value — the bank's numbers are what count for the loan.

02 What changes

Engine A

Gold Loan · BEAGLE v1.1

Branch-led gold loan — the everyday Federal flow.
  • Valuation gate removedThe Oro-side gold valuation step is gone from the loan flow — the customer lands directly on plan selection.
  • Bank appraisal sheet stepA new step where the partner records the bank appraiser sheet (manual now, image-read later) — the single mandatory valuation input.
  • Federal precheck UISequential per-check loader on the partner app; on failure, Screenshot + Copy-error only (no retry — terminal, auto-cancels the visit).
  • Loan purpose captureA new customer screen after plan confirmation — required before the loan can be submitted.
  • AA bank linkingThe Finvu AA SDK replaces manual account entry; unsupported banks fall back to saved-account selection.
  • AA-first fund confirmationFund transfer confirms automatically via AA when consent exists, with partner proof-upload as backup. AA always wins on completion source.
  • Reschedule & cancelCustomers can reschedule or cancel before the visit starts (amends the same visit, customer-only).
  • Ops: visits & queuesAdmin GL-visits refresh (5-path cancellation, loan-state-conflict reconciliation) and Atlas queues for API-failure & fund-transfer-pending.
Engine B

Takeover Engine v1.2

Moving a customer's gold loan to Oro: Offer → Bridge Loan (BRL) → Existing Lender Release (ELR) → Gold Loan.
  • Offer creation, revampedGuided, step-by-step offer with camera capture + auto-read replacing the old single manual form — across Customer, Partner, Admin, and Ringmaster.
  • BRL · AA bank linkingAfter the customer confirms the bridge-loan plan, the AA SDK opens and locks the payout account (saved-account fallback if unsupported).
  • BRL · Co-borrower eSignA unique eSign link is generated after the primary signs, sent by SMS and shareable from the partner app's share tray.
  • BRL · Check Payment StatusThe old "Change" button becomes Check Payment Status — AA-backed auto-check, with manual proof upload always available.
  • ELR · schedulingPer-lender by default, with combined-lender visits supported (gold photos per lender captured within the combined visit).
  • ELR · structure & trackingOne parent ELR ID per BRL visit; ELR visits shown as a tab in the BRL visit; searching an ELR ID lands on that tab.
  • BRL status extendedFour new statuses after ELR-scheduled: ELR in progress → gold in Cluster Office → ELR complete → GL visit scheduled (BV22–BV25).
  • ELR for the customerA new observational status view — the customer follows their gold being released, stored, and brought back.
  • Ops: BRL visits & loanLoan-Ops surfaces for BRL visit actions and loan actions (payment-proof review, AA approval).
Related change

Decoupled Appraisal separate PRD

Enables the valuation-gate removal above — Oro's own appraisal becomes its own module.
  • Out of the loan flowThe Oro valuation is removed from the gold-loan flow; the bank appraiser sheet is the only mandatory input. No more matching.
  • Standalone appraisal moduleA new partner-app module for Oro's own valuation — ornament-level capture (type, weight, purity, images, value), by phone number, with view / edit / re-appraise.
  • Optional loan linkA loan can link to an appraisal (from the loan flow) but it's informational — never a gate. Feeds part-release, top-up, settlement, and private-sale.
